Teaching

Pluralsight courses.

I write and record courses for Pluralsight: practical AI content for business professionals working with Microsoft technology. No filler, no padding. Both courses are short on purpose, because your time matters more than a course completion badge.

Course 01

Beginner

Prompt Engineering for Human Resources

Built for HR professionals, not developers. This course walks through writing clear, effective prompts for the work HR actually does: recruiting and job descriptions, employee communications, policy drafting, and performance conversations. No technical jargon, no theory for theory's sake.

You'll leave with prompt patterns you can reuse straight away, and a way of thinking about AI tools that holds up whichever one your organisation gives you.

Who it's for: HR teams and people managers using M365 Copilot, ChatGPT, or any generative AI tool in their day-to-day work.

Course 02

Intermediate

Navigating Societal Impacts of AI

AI is reshaping how we work, communicate, and make decisions. This course gives business professionals a grounded tour of the real societal implications: bias, transparency, and workforce impact, without the doom and without the hype.

At its heart it's a course about judgement: understanding where AI's blind spots are, so you can use it responsibly and speak credibly about it in your organisation.

Who it's for: Business professionals who use or sponsor AI and want to understand its wider impact, not just its features.
