About

The short version.

Microsoft MVP · M365 Copilot & Copilot Studio

I help organisations adopt AI without losing control of it.

I'm a Microsoft MVP in M365 Copilot and Copilot Studio and a Managing Consultant at Robiquity, an AI and automation consultancy. Day to day, I help organisations work out what's worth building with AI, how to govern it, and how to make it stick.

The centre of that work is AI governance and enablement. I design and build AI and Power Platform Centres of Excellence (environment strategy, DLP, ALM, agent lifecycle) alongside the enablement programmes, maker communities, and training that turn governance into an enabler rather than a blocker.

Away from client work, I'm a Pluralsight author with two published courses, a 15-time Microsoft certified professional, and co-founder of the Norfolk Power Platform User Group and the East of England Summit.

The work in practice

01
01

Centre of Excellence design

Standing up the team, processes, and tooling an organisation needs to run AI and Power Platform at scale, or getting an existing CoE unstuck. Environment strategy, ALM, and an operating model people will follow.

02

Copilot & agent lifecycle

Working through the detail with IT and security teams: who can build agents, which data and connectors they can reach, how they're reviewed and monitored, and how they're retired once they've served their purpose.

03

Enablement programmes

Training curricula, maker communities, hackathons, and champion networks. The platform is only half the job; the people using it are the other half, and they're the half that decides whether adoption sticks.
