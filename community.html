Community

Built for the community.

The Microsoft community is one of the best things about working in this space. I've co-founded two communities in the East of England. Here's where to find them.

Norfolk Power Platform User Group

01 · nppug.co.uk →

Connect, learn, share, and grow together. A community for Power Platform enthusiasts across Norwich and Norfolk, open to everyone from first-time makers to enterprise developers. We meet regularly at Townshend House, Norwich, covering Power Apps, Power Automate, Power BI, Copilot Studio, Dynamics 365, Dataverse, AI, Azure, and more. All events are free.
