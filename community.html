Why it matters

The Microsoft community is genuinely one of the best things about working in this space. I've learned more from community sessions and conversations than from any official training. The whole point of the Norfolk group and the Summit is to create that same experience for people in the region.

If you're working with Power Platform or Microsoft AI and you're in the East of England, come to an event. They're free, they're friendly, and you'll leave with something useful.
